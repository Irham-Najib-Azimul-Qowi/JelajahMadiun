## Bahasa Indonesia

Pahlawan Religi Centre (PRC) terletak di kawasan Sumber Umis, berdampingan dengan Pahlawan Street Center. Tempat ini menjadi wisata edukasi manasik haji dan wisata religi populer berkat replika Ka'bah, halaman seperti Masjidil Haram, serta Payung Madinah megah.

---

## English

Pahlawan Religi Centre (PRC) is located in the Sumber Umis area adjacent to PSC. It serves as a Hajj pilgrimage educational site and popular religious destination featuring a Kaaba replica and grand Medina umbrellas.
