## Bahasa Indonesia

Dawet Suronatan telah ada sejak tahun 1960-an. Memiliki isian unik berupa cendol hijau, ketan hitam, tape singkong, bubur sumsum, sirup gula jawa asli, dan kuah santan gurih.

---

## English

Dawet Suronatan has operated since the 1960s. Features a unique bowl filled with green jelly, black glutinous rice, cassava tape, rice marrow porridge, palm sugar syrup, and coconut milk.
